Delete your Finkuu account

Finkuu (package in.finzen.app, developer: Vivek) lets you permanently delete your account and all associated data, directly inside the app.

Delete in the app (instant)

  1. Open Finkuu and go to Profile → Settings
  2. Under Data, tap “Delete account & all data”
  3. Confirm with Delete forever

What gets deleted

  • Your sign-in account (email)
  • Your encrypted cloud backup (accounts, transactions, plans, investments)
  • Any feedback you submitted with your email attached
  • All data stored locally on your device

Deletion is immediate and irreversible. No data is retained after deletion.

Can't access the app?

Email vivekvice40@gmail.com from your registered email address with the subject “Finkuu account deletion request”. Your account and data will be deleted within 7 days and you'll receive a confirmation.
