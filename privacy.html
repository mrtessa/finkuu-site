Finkuu — Privacy Policy

Effective: 6 June 2026 · App: Finkuu (in.finzen.app) · Contact: vivekvice40@gmail.com

Finkuu is a personal finance tracker built on one principle: your money data belongs to you and stays with you.

What we collect

  • Email address — used only to sign you in (one-time passcodes) and to key your cloud backup.
  • Financial entries you create — accounts, balances, transactions, bills, income plans and investments. Stored on your device; optionally backed up, encrypted in transit, to our database (Supabase) so you can restore after reinstalling.
  • Feedback you choose to send — star rating and message, with your email.

Notification access (optional)

If you enable Auto-detect payments, Finkuu reads notifications on your device to find bank and UPI payment alerts and pre-fill transactions for you. It also reads Finkuu's own sign-in code emails to auto-fill them at login. This processing happens entirely on your phone. Notification content is never transmitted off the device, never stored beyond the detected transaction you approve, and non-financial notifications are ignored and discarded. You can disable this anytime in Settings → Auto-detect payments, or in Android's Notification access settings.

What we never do

  • No selling or sharing of your data with anyone
  • No advertising, no trackers, no analytics SDKs profiling you
  • No access to your bank accounts — Finkuu only knows what you enter or approve

Where data lives

Primary storage is your device. Cloud backup and sign-in are provided by Supabase (project hosted region: as configured). Google Sign-In, if you use it, is governed by Google's privacy policy.

Deleting your data

Profile → Delete my account & data erases your account, cloud backup, feedback and local data immediately and irreversibly. Details: account deletion page.

Children

Finkuu is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

Material changes to this policy will be announced in the app before they take effect.
